Rabies vaccination after exposure to an infected animal

 Rabies vaccine for friends who have been exposed to an infected animal.
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Friends who have been exposed to an infected animal, or there is a suspicion that it is infected.

 What is the entitlement?
Rabies vaccine.

For more privileges on this topic
Rabies Prevention Vaccine for Workers with Animals

How much does it cost?
No cost.

Where is the service provided?
