Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Silver friends who have had neurological damage to the upper limbs.

What is the entitlement?
One splint for the same organ in a calendar year, with a deductible payment from the supplier in agreement with Maccabi.
In a calendar year, it is possible to exercise for the same organ only one accessory - a neurological brace or an orthopedic accessory.

What should you do?
A valid referral is required from a Maccabi doctor, a hospital doctor, a private doctor, a home heart doctor or a Maccabi dormitory doctor in one of the following areas: neurology, pediatric neurology, orthopedics, rehabilitation, occupational therapy or physical therapy.
Contact the supplier. The provider will conduct an eligibility check, and if a beneficiary is found, the transaction will be made by telephone and a credit card deductible or bank transfer will be charged.
After payment, the supplier will send the brace to your home by courier.
If you are entitled to medical treatment or reimbursement or participation for medical expenses under the following laws, the service will not be provided under the additional health care services:
 
HURT IN HOSTILITIES RECOGNIZED BY SOCIAL SECURITY
Disabled Ministry of Defense 20% or more
Injured in road accidents
 
If the need for the service is not due to the violation recognized by the laws listed above, a letter from a doctor confirming this must be submitted to the office of the medical center (branch), in order to obtain permission for the realization of eligibility.
The letter can be submitted in an envelope to a queue or sent by registered mail or email to the medical center. Locating a Medical Center >
