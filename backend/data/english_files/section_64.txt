What else is worth knowing?
A friend who visited an outpatient clinic of the same profession several times in the same quarter - will be charged one quarterly deductible.
A member who visited an outpatient clinic of the same profession, in the same quarter, but at different hospitals - will be charged a quarterly deductible for each of the hospitals individually.
A member who applied to outpatient clinics in different professions in the same quarter - will be charged quarterly deductibles for each clinic individually.
