The service is provided by suppliers by agreement

How much does it cost?
Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Shaliam on the doctor's referral it is not explicitly stated that your medical condition requires taking blood only at home - 50₪ deductible. BASKET BASICWITH MEDICAL NEED: FREE OF CHARGE.
Where is the service provided?
At the friend's house.

What should you do?
Members whose medical condition requires taking blood from home should receive a referral from the attending physician who specifically states that your medical condition requires taking blood at home only
When the referral is produced, it is automatically transmitted to the laboratory, and from the laboratory you will be contacted to arrange for Maccabi Silver, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Shelli to receive a doctor's referral for blood tests.
Fill out the registration form below, and the relevant laboratory will contact you to arrange the date of the test and provide instructions.
The payment will be charged by phone to the credit card by the provider in the agreement, after the inspection has been carried out.
 
It is important to know that blood sampling will be carried out within 5 working days from the transfer of the referral to the laboratory.
Tests that can not be done at home:Sugar loading
First and second trimester examinations (fetal protein and occipital transparency)
Hypercoagulability tests
Purpurine, aluminum, lactic acid, pyruvic acid, growth hormone (GH) tests, renin and aldosterone tests, test for VITC, homocysteine, cryophibrinogen and cryoglobulin tests
Cytology and pathology tests
Growth hormone (GH) All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
