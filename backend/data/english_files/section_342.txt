Egg donation

 Infertility treatment designed for women who are unable to produce high-quality eggs on their own. In the process, donor eggs are extracted, fertilizing them in a sperm cell, and the developed embryos are returned to the donated uterus.

What should you do?
Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold
Consult a licensed fertility doctor (s).
It is the responsibility of the doctor to forward the application on a form directly to the fertility certification center, and to receive the answer from them.
It is the responsibility of the doctor to give you the answer of the Center for Fertility Certifications up to 7 working days from the date of opening the application. 
To get a refund for the donation of the egg and its fertilization, you must apply for a refund on the Maccabi Online website or in the application and attach the required documents.
 
If necessary, the documents can also be submitted at an appointment at the medical center or sent by registered mail to the medical center.
Required documents:original tax receipt and invoice from the country or abroad.
Stage III Treatment Summary Form (Medical Summary of Return of Embryos).
