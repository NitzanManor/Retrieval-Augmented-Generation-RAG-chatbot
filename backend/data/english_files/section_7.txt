Pre-implantation diagnosis for women in IVF treatments

 Diagnosis of the presence of a genetic disease in a fertilized egg outside the uterus, before rooting it in the woman's uterus.
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Women up to their 45th birthday who are undergoing IVF treatments in Maccabi, with the recommendation of a genetic counselor.
Eligibility is limited to 2 children who have ended up with the birth of healthy children using this technology (regardless of the number of fetuses in each pregnancy or the number of children born before diagnosis).

What is the entitlement?
Pre-implantation diagnosis without limitation of the number of oocytes, in accordance with medical indications in the health basket.

What should you do?
