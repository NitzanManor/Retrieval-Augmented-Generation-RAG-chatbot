Vaginal trainer for oncology patient

 A medical accessory that treats the phenomenon of vaginal narrowing as a result of radiation treatments in the pelvic area.
 

Last updated: 27.6.22
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Oncological patients from Maccabi who underwent local radiation therapy in the pelvic area.

What is the entitlement?
Cash Back On Purchase Of Up To 2 Vaginal Trainers Only (Lifetime).
