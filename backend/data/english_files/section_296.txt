NEUROLOGICAL PHYSIOTHERAPY

 Treatments to rehabilitate the locomotor system due to damage to the nervous system, as in cases of rehabilitation from a stroke, brain tumors, spinal cord injury, car accidents and other traumas.
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Friends suffering from damage to the nervous system.

What is the entitlement?
Preliminary diagnosis and physiotherapy treatments, depending on the need and professional judgment, which include varied manual techniques and exercises and are focused on a functional goal, such as achieving independent mobility and restoring gait.
