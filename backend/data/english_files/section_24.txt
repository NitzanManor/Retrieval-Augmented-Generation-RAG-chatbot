What is the entitlement?
A series of 12 personal fitness workouts
or
A series of 12 fitness trainings in groups of 3 participants
 
You can convert up to 2 workouts in 2 sessions with a nutritionist
The duration of the workout is about an hour, at your home or in a public place (beach, park, etc.) and not in a gym.

What else is worth knowing?
Eligibility for members aged 18 and over only.
Eligibility is granted once and is not renewed.
You must be a member of “Maccabi Shelli” for the entire training period. If you left the service for any reason, you will not be able to continue training.
A training session can be canceled or rescheduled directly in front of the company, up to 12 hours before the workout. No refund will be given for deductibles paid.
In group training - if one of the trainees does not make it to part of the training - no refund will be given for the deductible paid.
- According to the instructions of the Ministry of Health, when registering for the service, the trainee will be asked to fill out a health declaration. If necessary, it will be necessary to present a certificate from the attending physician to the fact that there is no medical prophylaxis for fitness training.
Approval of the training program will be granted subject to the statement of health and professional considerations of the service provider.
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
