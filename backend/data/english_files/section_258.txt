Facial Marital Surgery

 FACIAL MARRYING IS AN ACCOMPANYING SURGERY TO THE GENDER REASSIGNMENT PROCESS RESULTING FROM DIFFERENCES IN THE BONY STRUCTURE OF THE SKULL THAT EXIST BETWEEN A MAN AND A WOMAN.
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Transgender Maccabi members over the age of 18 who have received approval of the Public Committee for Sex Change Surgery.

What should you do?
