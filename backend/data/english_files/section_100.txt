Halachic Observance of Fertility Treatments

 A service designed for spouses in the process of in vitro fertilization (IVF) and allows close walking supervision in hospitals and fertility laboratories of the fertilization process.
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Zahav
Who is eligible?
Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi are friends with the IVF process, for one of the spouses, even if both spouses are Maccabi friends.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is provided to members of Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Shelli after 12 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
Up to 3 days of observation per treatment experience.
Phase A + B (IVF cycle — hormone therapy + egg pumping, fertilization with sperm cells and returning them to the uterus) are considered to be one therapeutic trial.

How much does it cost?
Refund up to the ceiling of NIS 135.85 per day supervision.

What should you do?
Submit to the medical center by registered mail or by post to queue an original receipt and tax invoice containing the name and ID number of the eligible spouse, and the number of days of observation and for which treatment experience the service is provided. After receiving the documents, a pledge will be received.

What else is worth knowing?
This entitlement is not valid for the case of insemination (IUI).
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
