What should you do?
If you are conducting a delayed system review within Maccabi, you should bring a referral from a Maccabi women's doctor.
If you perform the examination in a private setting, to receive a refund, you must submit an original tax receipt and invoice in the application and in the Maccabi Online at the following link, or submit to the medical center by registered mail or queue receipt and original tax invoice and a medical summary letter from the performing doctor (s).

What else is worth knowing?
Girls up to 19 years old who perform gynecological ultrasound are exempt from payment.
Eligibility is for one pregnancy review.
If the doctor conducting the review is advised by “Maccabi Zahav” and “Review+Consultation” is listed on the invoice, a refund will be approved only for the review.
Only if the pregnancy was terminated for a medical or natural reason, the company would be eligible for another examination in the same insurance year.
If you chose a late review in a private setting, you cannot also do an early review in a private setting.
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
