Where is the service provided?
At Maccabi Pharm
In a private pharmacy
In any store that sells silicone sheets

What should you do? GET A REFERRAL FROM A DOCTOR FROM ONE OF THESE SPECIALTIES: ORTHOPEDICS, PLASTIC SURGERY, OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY, DERMATOLOGY OR RECONSTRUCTIVE MEDICINE. The referral will include a medical diagnosis and a recommendation to purchase the accessory.
Contact with the referral to an occupational therapy institute. 
At the Institute of Occupational Therapy, they will adapt the sheet to your needs, filling out a dedicated form in which the type of sheet and the quantity needed are indicated.
To purchase the sheets, the referral must be presented at the pharmacy, paid, and then contact Maccabi for a refund.- At Maccabi Pharm - a deductible will be paid - in any other store - Full price must be paid
Refund - must be delivered to the medical center, at an appointment post or by registered mail, original receipt and tax invoice. 
With each purchase, all these steps must be repeated anew.
