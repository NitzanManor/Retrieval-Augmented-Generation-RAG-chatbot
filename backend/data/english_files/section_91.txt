Termination of pregnancy in a private setting

 Maccabi Shelli girlfriends can have a pregnancy termination at a private doctor in a private hospital. The action is conditional on the approval of the Committee on Termination of Pregnancy.
 

Last updated: 20.9.22
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli

Who is eligible?
Maccabi Shelli girlfriends who have the approval of the commission for termination of pregnancy. Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli membership after 6 months from the date of joining the program.

Where is the service provided?
At a private doctor in a private hospital.

What should you do?
The exercise of eligibility is conditional on the approval of the commission for termination of pregnancy. To the list of committees for the approval of termination of pregnancy
 The doctor must be paid privately
What else is worth knowing?
In the case of a fetus without a pulse - no commission for termination of pregnancy is required. The attending gynecologist will refer you directly to a hospital.
