What is the entitlement?
Maccabi Shelli
3 nights in a sanatorium for each child from the first to the third child.
At the birth of a fourth child and later - if a refund has been received from Maccabi Gold, you can receive a refund for 3 additional nights of convalescence within the framework of Maccabi Shelli.
There is no obligation to first utilize entitlements from Maccabi Gold.
The days of convalescence do not have to be consecutive.
Maccabi Gold
After the birth of a fourth child - up to 3 consecutive convalescent nights
AFTER THE BIRTH OF A FIFTH CHILD ONWARDS - FROM 15.5.2023, AN ADDITIONAL DAY OF CONVALESCENCE WILL BE PROVIDED, DEPENDING ON THE NUMBER OF CHILDREN AND WITHOUT LIMITATION OF THE NUMBER OF BIRTHS. FOR EXAMPLE:AFTER THE BIRTH OF A FIFTH CHILD - UP TO 4 CONSECUTIVE SANITATION NIGHTS
After the birth of a sixth child - up to 5 consecutive convalescent nights

Hotels in hospitals

What should you do?
For a refund, you must apply for a refund on the Maccabi Online website or in the app and attach the required documents.
If necessary, the documents can also be submitted at an appointment at the medical center or sent by registered mail to the medical center.
 
Required documents:
Original receipt and tax invoice from the sanatorium with an indication of the dates of stay at the place or original receipt and tax invoice are accompanied by a letter from the sanatorium, indicating the dates of stay at the place.
If the newborn is not yet registered with Maccabi, a letter of release from the maternity home must also be presented.
The refund is also conditional on the approval of proper book management of the supplier and thus that the field of employment with the tax authorities corresponds to the subject of the service in the refund.

What else is worth knowing? Eligibility is granted only to the maternity.
No refund will be given for a spouse who has stayed in the sanatorium.
If the spouse stayed with you in the sanatorium, it is necessary to present an original tax receipt and invoice in which the price for an individual person in the room with the addition of a chaperone is indicated.
In the event that a separate tax receipt and invoice are not presented to the lender, a refund will be made in the amount of 85% of the invoice amount up to the ceiling established in the entitlement 
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
