Home accessories for the age of 65 and older

 Home accessories are designed for those who need help with daily functioning in the home environment, such as bathroom and shower aids, mobility aids and kitchen.
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli
Who is eligible?
Maccabi members are 65 years of age or older.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli members after 6 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
 
Purchase of home accessories at a reduced cost up to a cumulative ceiling of 1,134.39₪ for three years.
Eligibility is renewed after three years from the date of first exercise.
One accessory of any type can be purchased once every 3 years, except for outposts on which there is no restriction.
The benefit is given in the form of a cover check.

Where is the service provided?
In any store that sells the accessory.

What should you do?
GET A REFERRAL FROM A MACCABI STAFF MEMBER IN ONE OF THE FOLLOWING AREAS: THERAPIST, NURSE, PHYSIOTHERAPIST OR OCCUPATIONAL CLINICIAN, PRIOR TO PURCHASE. The referral should include a medical diagnosis and a recommendation for the purchase of the accessory. Submit the following documents to the medical center by registered mail or by appointment: The referral you received from a Maccabi staff member.
Original tax receipt and invoice.After receiving the documents the refund will be sent to you.
(The refund is also conditional on the approval of the supplier's proper book management and thus that the field of employment with the tax authorities corresponds to the subject of the service in the refund).
If you are entitled to medical treatment or reimbursement or participation for medical expenses under the following laws, the service will not be provided under the additional health care services:
 
HURT IN HOSTILITIES RECOGNIZED BY SOCIAL SECURITY
Disabled Ministry of Defense 20% or more
Injured in road accidents
 
If the need for the service is not due to the violation recognized by the laws listed above, a letter from a doctor confirming this must be submitted to the office of the medical center (branch), in order to obtain permission for the realization of eligibility.
The letter can be submitted in an envelope to a queue or sent by registered mail or email to the medical center. Locating a Medical Center >
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
