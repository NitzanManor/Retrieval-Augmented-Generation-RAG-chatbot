Surgery and special treatments abroad (excluding transplants)

 Maccabi members are entitled to life-saving surgeries and treatments, in accordance with the procedures, regulations and participation rates established by law, and subject to prior approval from Maccabi. 
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Zahav
Who is eligible?
Maccabi Shelli Gold and Maccabi members who meet one of these criteria:
 
Waiting for surgery/treatment in the country can cause a worsening of the member's medical condition or risk to his life. This is provided that the waiting time abroad is shorter than the waiting time for surgery/treatment in Israel
There is a minority of experience in performing the surgery/treatment in Israel
The surgery/treatment cannot be performed in Israel, or no alternative surgery is performed in Israel
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold members after 24 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
Reimbursement up to a ceiling of $150,000. 
The reimbursement ceiling includes:ancillary expenses - accompanying, flying, staying, and flying expenses of the member's body if he or she is not allowed to die.
Medical expenses, including reimbursement of the implant as necessary (detailed in the section “How much it costs”).

in hospitals abroad.

What should you do?
 
For permission for surgery abroad, an application must be submitted in advance, accompanied by the following documents:
Application form for receiving medical services abroad, filled and signed. Including, Signing a Confidentiality Waiver Form
Medical recommendation to perform the operation abroad
Documents from the Overseas Medical Center Proving the Friend Was Accepted for Surgery
bid
Summons for analysis
 
Submission of documents:
The documents must be submitted to the medical center via e-mail, the Maccabi app or by contacting the center directly.
 
Confirmation of receipt of the referral:
Upon receipt of the referral at the Department of Overseas Surgery in Maccabi, you will be sent an email confirming that the referral has been received, along with information about the process of handling the referral.
 
Making a decision regarding the appeal:
After making a decision regarding your application, a letter will be sent to you indicating whether the application has been approved or denied. 
If the application is approved - the letter will contain additional instructions for the continuation of the process.

Hurt by hostilities recognized by Social Security Car accident victim: exemption from surcharges and self-participation
Accident at work recognized by Social Security
A member recognized as a victim of traffic accidents/enemies/incapacitated Ministry of Defense who needs accessories/services regardless of the limb injured in the accident - is entitled to participation/reimbursement from the SHB according to the rules of the regulations. A medical letter must be submitted for the approval of the Shabun administration in the district indicating that the need for accessories/services is not due to the recognized damage. 
 
If the need for the service is not due to the violation recognized by the laws listed above, a letter from a doctor confirming this must be submitted to the office of the medical center (branch), in order to obtain permission for the realization of eligibility.
The letter can be submitted in an envelope to a queue or sent by registered mail or email to the medical center. Locating a Medical Center >
