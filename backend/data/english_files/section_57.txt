Bone density test without medical diagnosis

 A major diagnostic tool for osteoporosis, a disease manifested by a decrease in bone density and a familiar tendency to fractures and cracks in the bones.
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver
Who is eligible?
Maccabi Shelli friends, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Silver.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi Gold members after 12 months from the date of joining the program.

How much does it cost?
For examination of one limb (spine or femur): 165.96₪ deductible
For examination of two organs (spine and hip): 228.20₪ deductible

Where is the service provided?
AT THE MEDICAL CENTERS OF ASUTA

What should you do?
You must stock up on the following documents (printed)
Referral of a doctor
Maccabi Card
ID
