Restoration of the nipple crown using a tattoo - on an oncological background

 Complementary procedure in case of need for breast or nipple reconstruction surgery on an oncological background.
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver
Who is eligible?
Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Silver companies after breast or nipple reconstruction surgery performed on an oncological background.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi Gold members after 12 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
Surgery to restore the crown of the nipple through tattooing.

How much does it cost?
No cost. The cost was included in the self-paid participation for breast reconstruction surgery. 

Where is the service provided?
With a specialist for restorative micropigmentation in agreement with Maccabi.
To locate an expert

What should you do?
Get a referral from a plastic surgeon.
Schedule an appointment with an agreed therapist.
