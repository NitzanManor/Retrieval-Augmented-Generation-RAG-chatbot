Electric Field Therapy Kit for Brain Cancer Diagnostics

 Treatment of electrical fields in brain cancer (GBM).
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Diagnosed with brain cancer (glioblastoma multiforme) over 18 years of age, after surgery and combination therapy with radiation and adjuvant therapy with Temozolomide, combined maintenance therapy with Temozolomide and after discontinuation of Temozolomide maintenance therapy.

What is the entitlement?
Treatment with electric fields in accordance with the prior approval of the Maccabi.

How much does it cost?
No cost.
