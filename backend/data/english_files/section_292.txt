Wig for patients with alopecia areata totalis/Universalis 

 A wig that helps to cope aesthetically and mentally with the effects of alopecia areata disease, which causes hair loss.
 

Last updated: 6/30/22
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Maccabi members who have one of these diagnoses:
Alopecia areata totalis
Alopecia areata universalis

What is the entitlement?
1 wig - once every 2 years floating.
