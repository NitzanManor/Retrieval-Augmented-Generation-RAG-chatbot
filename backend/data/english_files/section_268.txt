Custom Orthopedic Shoes

 Shoes of individual tailoring or special construction in a workshop, for friends with structural deformation of the foot or ankle. Combined with inner insole and external or internal elevation as needed.
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver
Who is eligible?
Members of Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Silver who are not eligible for participation by the Ministry of Health, or who are eligible for partial participation, will receive reimbursement or completion of reimbursement from Maccabi in the following cases:
 
Patients with rheumatic disease with a marked deformity of the foot.
Diabetics with fingerlessness, peripheral nerve damage, history of ulcers, predisposition to ulcers, blood flow disturbance or necrosis, foot deformity.
Patients after partial resection of the foot.
Muscle paralysis.
A person entitled to the Ministry of Health who exercised his entitlement in the current year, and the following year purchased shoes at full price, is entitled to a refund through Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold or Maccabi Silver.
A beneficiary of the Ministry of Health who has exercised his entitlement in the current year and has paid a deductible, is entitled to receive reimbursement from Maccabi for the deductible paid at the Ministry of Health. The refund from Maccabi will be made according to the eligibility conditions set out below.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi Gold members after 12 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
One pair of orthopedic shoes per calendar year.
For those eligible on behalf of the Ministry of Health, eligibility is once every two years.

How much does it cost? Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi ZehavA refund of 83% of the actual expenditure up to a ceiling of 743.25₪.
No refund will be given for an accessory purchased abroad or ordered from abroad. 
Maccabi Silver refund of 33% of the actual expenditure and up to a ceiling of 295.69₪.
No refund will be given for an accessory purchased abroad or ordered from abroad. 
Where is the service provided?
In all orthopedic stores.
