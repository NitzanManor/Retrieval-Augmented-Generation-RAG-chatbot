Placental cicatricial test for pregnant women over 33

 An examination that is carried out in the first trimester of pregnancy and is used to identify genetic diseases in the fetus.
 

Last updated: 01.1.24
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Membership over 33 years of age (by date of birth) at the time of becoming pregnant, and whose date of entry into pregnancy applies after 01.02.2023, if both conditions are met together
The test is designed for companies who are in the first trimester of pregnancy, at 10-13 weeks
 Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli members after 6 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
Placental cicatricial examination is funded by the Ministry of Health, in accordance with the recommendation of a genetic counselor.

Where is the service provided?
In public hospitals.
Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi Gold companies can perform the test in a private hospital or private institute or at a private doctor.

What should you do?
 
If the genetic counselor has determined that the examination will be funded by the Ministry of Health, you must make an appointment at one of the public hospitals.
For testing in a private framework - Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi Zahav Companies
Submit to the medical center by registered mail or stand to queue receipt and original tax invoice to receive a refund. A doctor's referral is not required.
Applying for a refund in the Applikation and on the Maccabi Online website

What else is worth knowing?
Maccabi Shelli companies that are not eligible for the examination under the basic basket can perform it privately (at the Asuta Hospital, for example), as part of the Pregnancy and Childbirth Package Benefit >
Placental cicatricial examination is a substitute for amniocentesis. As part of Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi Zahav, you can only pass one of the 2 tests.
NO REIMBURSEMENT WILL BE GIVEN FOR A PLACENTAL SCISY EXAMINATION WHICH WILL BE PERFORMED BY A MACCABI DOCTOR. It is recommended that the company check this before performing the test.
THIS ENTITLEMENT IS ALSO GRANTED TO COMPANIES THAT ARE ELIGIBLE FOR THE EXAMINATION UNDER THE HEALTH BASKET BUT WISH TO CARRY OUT THE EXAMINATION IN A PRIVATE FRAMEWORK.
No refund will be given for an inspection carried out abroad. 
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
