Occipital transparency test 

 A test to detect an increased risk of having a baby with chromosomal abnormalities such as Down syndrome. Conducted at the 11-14th week of pregnancy.
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Membership in the first trimester of pregnancy.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli members after 6 months from the date of joining the program.

What should you do?
Get a referral from a women's doctor.
Fill in the form and sign the doctor (s) on two copies: Download a declaration form for the subject >
To receive a refund for an examination in a private setting, you must submit to the medical center by registered mail or by mail to the queue an original receipt and tax invoice, which include your name clearly and explicitly.

What else is worth knowing?
Women up to the age of 19 are eligible for a free check-up.
No refund will be given for deductibles paid for testing in a private framework.
In each request for a refund, the month and year of pregnancy must be explicitly stated.
