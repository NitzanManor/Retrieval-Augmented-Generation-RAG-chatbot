Removing glasses with a laser from the age of 18 and older

 A cosmetic-surgical procedure designed to correct vision and relieve the surgeon from the need to wear glasses or contact lenses. The procedure is performed using a thin cut in a laser beam.
 

Last updated: 3/14/23
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli
Who is eligible?
Maccabi Shelli friends from the age of 18 who are interested in surgery to remove glasses with a laser.
Seniority required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli members after 6 months from the date of joining the program.

What is the entitlement?
Reduced deductible for the cost of laser surgery to remove glasses. The operation can be performed on two dates, if one eye is operated on each date.
 
Eligibility is exercisable for one membership period.

What should you do?
Before surgery 
For information about the operation, a consultation appointment and suitability for surgery - contact the institutes under the agreement. 
It is necessary to arrive at the clinic with a Maccabi card or ID. 
FIND OUT IN THE CLINIC ABOUT THE PROCESS, INCLUDING PREOPERATIVE EXAMINATION, WHERE THE EXAMINATION IS PERFORMED AND WHERE THE OPERATION IS ACTUALLY PERFORMED. Full information about the operation and adjustment to the condition of the eyes will be given in the clinic.
Pay directly to the institute the deductible only by type of surgery. No refund will be given for the deductible paid.
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
