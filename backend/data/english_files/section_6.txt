Diagnosis of ADHD and hyperactivity disorder with a doctor
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Children and adults from 6 years and older.
(CHILDREN UNDER 6 YEARS OF AGE ARE ENTITLED TO EXERCISE THE RIGHT AT THE INSTITUTES OF CHILD DEVELOPMENT).
What is the entitlement?
Diagnosis of ADHD and hyperactivity disorder in children and adults.

Additional entitlements in this regard
Computerized Test for Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorders
Complementary therapies for children with ADHD

How much does it cost?
In mental health clinics: no cost
Certified pediatricians or family doctors, neurologists or psychiatrists - a quarterly deductible of NIS 30.50 will be charged
