What else is worth knowing?
TREATMENT OF NATURAL MACCABIES BEGINS WITH A MEDICAL CONSULTATION, DURING WHICH AN INDIVIDUAL TREATMENT PLAN IS BUILT FOR YOU ACCORDING TO YOUR CONDITION AND NEEDS. Treatments will only be approved after medical advice.
Medical advice is considered treatment and is included in the benefit of 20 treatments per year for members of Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Shelli
Payment for treatments will be charged in advance
It is worth bringing to the first appointment in the clinic any relevant medical material: MRI, CT, X-rays, results of laboratory tests, letters of discharge from a hospital, etc.
Eligibility is conditional on the member remaining on the same plan for the entire length of the treatment period
The prices of treatments are according to the Maccabi Natural Price List, subject to the additional health services regulations of Maccabi Health Services and the updates of the index. T.L.H.
MEDICAL MASSAGE AND HERBAL ADVICE IN ACUPUNCTURE TREATMENT ARE EXCLUDED FROM THE ELIGIBILITY OF MACCABI ZAHU
If you are entitled to medical treatment or reimbursement or participation for medical expenses under the following laws, the service will not be provided under the additional health care services:
 
Policeman injured in the line of duty
HURT IN HOSTILITIES RECOGNIZED BY SOCIAL SECURITY
Disabled Ministry of Defense 20% or more
Injured in road accidents
Injured in work accident
 
If the need for the service is not due to the violation recognized by the laws listed above, a letter from a doctor confirming this must be submitted to the office of the medical center (branch), in order to obtain permission for the realization of eligibility.
The letter can be submitted in an envelope to a queue or sent by registered mail or email to the medical center. Locating a Medical Center >
