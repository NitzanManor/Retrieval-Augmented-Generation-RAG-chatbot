Blood allergy test

 A blood test that examines allergic reactions in the blood to various substances in food, inhaled substances, and more.

Maccabi Shelli friends, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Silver.

What is the entitlement?
Test for the diagnosis of allergic reactions to a variety of substances, as indicated in the referral to the allergist.
 
Learn more about allergy testing

How much does it cost?
Deductible of 228₪ per referral (regardless of the number of tests required).

Where is the service provided?
At Maccabi Laboratories
Payment for the test will be made at the Maccabi offices either frontally or by telephone

What should you do?
get a referral from an allergist. Summons for a doctor's appointment
Make an appointment for the examination at the laboratory or medical center of Maccabi.

What else is worth knowing?
Eligibility for inspection is a unique entitlement for Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Silver members. Anyone who is not a member of one of these insurance plans will not be able to take the test, even at full price.
Payment is per referral, regardless of the amount of allergens listed in the referral or the number of tests.
New invocation will require a re-deductible payment.
Antihistamines (any antiallergic drugs) should be avoided for 7 days before the test. TAKING ANTIHISTAMINES DOES NOT ALLOW PERFORMING SKIN OBSERVATIONS TO CLARIFY ALLERGY.
