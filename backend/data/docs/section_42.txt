Periodic screening tests for those aged 45 and over

 Reduced-cost medical check-up package for members aged 45 and older.

Who is eligible?
Maccabi Shelli friends are from 45 years old or older.
Seniority required in the insurance plan

What is the entitlement?
Survey testing package once every 3 years, which includes: 
Doctor examination
Blood and urine test
Blood pressure test
ECG test
Lung function
Chest scan (for smokers only)
Vision test
Intraocular pressure test
Fecal occult blood test
Hearing test
Eligibility includes one of the following tests, of your choice:
Ergometry
Mammography
Gynecological examination (including breast examination)
Bone density test

How much does it cost?
25.59₪ deductible for the whole package.
