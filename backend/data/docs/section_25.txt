Collection and preservation of cord blood for Maccabi Shelli companies

 Blood extracted from the umbilical cord of the newborn and from the placenta, which is a source of stem cells, which can be used to treat a variety of diseases.

What is the entitlement?
Participation in the expenses of taking umbilical cord blood, its maintenance and preservation for 15 years.

What should you do?
Prenatal
During pregnancy, you must contact the provider for the service.
The provider will email you an agreement, charge a registration fee and send a cord blood collection kit.
During childbirth
Contact the supplier immediately after delivery to determine the time of collection.
Representatives of the company will come to the hospital to collect an assessment.

What else is worth knowing?
Eligibility is renewed at each birth.
Eligibility is additionally on the pregnancy and childbirth basket, and not included in it.
In a multi-fetal birth, the deductible is the same as in the birth of one baby.
The terms of payment and collection of the deductible are made in direct coordination of the parents with the provider.
All of the above is subject to the additional health care regulations in Maccabi.
