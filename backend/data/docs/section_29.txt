Hospitalization before and after childbirth

 Every birth mother is entitled to 3 days of postpartum hospitalization funded by Social Security. IN SOME CASES, THE MATERNITY IS ENTITLED TO FUNDING ADDITIONAL OUTPATIENT DAYS.
The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver, Basic Basket
Who is eligible?
Prenatal pregnancies.

What is the entitlement?
3 days of hospitalization after childbirth at the expense of social security.
In cases where prenatal hospitalization is required, hospitalization for up to 3 days will be financed at the expense of social security.
Every extra day of hospitalization is at the expense of Maccabi.
