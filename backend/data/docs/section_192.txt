 A custom accessory is an accessory whose preparation involves custom sewing or special construction in a workshop

The insurance layers are: Maccabi Shelli, Maccabi Gold, Maccabi Silver
What is the entitlement?
Only one accessory per limb (for example, one per hand), once a calendar year with agreed suppliers.Seniority is required in the insurance plan
The service is given to Maccabi Shelli and Maccabi Gold members after 12 months from the date of joining the program.

What should you do?
A valid prescription is required from a doctor with the required specialization in accordance with the required accessory. ON THE PRESCRIPTION SHOULD BE EXPLICITLY INDICATED “CUSTOM ACCESSORY”.
Home heart doctors or Maccabi dormitory doctors can give a referral for all accessories.
The provider must be reached with a Maccabi card/ ID card/to open a “visit without a card” service in the Maccabi app, on the Maccabi Online website or at the call center *3555 (extension 8 from a mobile phone registered in Maccabi).

What else is worth knowing?
Before purchasing, it is necessary to contact the suppliers with an agreement to find out by phone the hours of operation.
